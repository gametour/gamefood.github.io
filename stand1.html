Workshop allowing participants to create their own bentos in the effigy of their favorite games.

Genshin Impact

Second Stand

dStand bringing together all the flagship recipes of MiHoYo's game “Genshin Impact” there will be as much wild boar as confusing Asian dishes.

Zelda

Third Stand

Not being grouped with the Nintendo Global stand, because the Zelda game has its own recipes as much to warm the body as to have refreshing things.

Warcraft

Fourth Stand

Cube recipes at will for minecraft fans, enough to give flavor to all these cubes.
